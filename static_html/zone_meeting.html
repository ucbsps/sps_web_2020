Society of Physics Students Zone 18 Meeting

Friday April 9th and Saturday April 10th

Events

Speakers

There will be three speaker sessions at the meeting.

Professor James Analytis will give our keynote speech on Friday from 5:30PM to 6:00PM PDT.

From 10:00AM to 11:00AM PDT on Saturday, Professors Raphael Bousso, Ori Ganor, Chung Pei, Mariska Kriek, Jessica Lu, and Courtney Dressing will speak.

From 2:00PM to 3:00PM PDT on Saturday, you may hear from Professors Naomi Ginsberg, Alessandra Lanzara, Francis Hellman, and Na Ji.

On Saturday, at 7:00PM PDT, there will be closing remarks.

Poster Sessions

We will have two poster sessions, A and B. Poster session A, on Saturday morning from 11:00AM to 12:00PM PDT, will feature posters on astrophysics, particle physics, and geophyiscs. On Saturday afternoon from 3:00PM to 4:00PM PDT, poster session B will take place, showing the latest research in biophysics, AMO, and quantum information.

Please sign up to present a poster here.

Panel

The zone meeting will feature a panel discussing post-undergraduate activities. Alumni in both industry and graduate school will be available to answer your questions! The panel will take place from 1:00PM to 2:00PM PDT on Saturday.

Workshops

Workshops at the zone meeting will be a valuable learning opportunity. Our first workshop, from 4:00PM to 5:00PM PDT on Saturday, will provide information on preparing a resume. After that, another workshop will take place from 5:00PM to 6:00PM PDT on Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion.

Socials

Finally, the zone meeting will have opportunities to relax and meet fellow physicists from zone 18. On Friday, we will have a Fermi Questions activity from 6:30PM to 7:30PM PDT. Another social event will take place on Saturday from 6:00PM to 7:00PM PDT.
