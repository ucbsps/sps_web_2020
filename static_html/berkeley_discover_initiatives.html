Berkeley Discover Initiatives

What is Berkeley Discover? A grant to improve your experience as a physics undergraduate! Opportunities include tutors for your classes, study halls to help you through your intro physics courses, mentorship programs, and financial support for club activities. One example is giving an Undergraduate Seminar, a Society of Physics Students event.
