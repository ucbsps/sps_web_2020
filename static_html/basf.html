SPS at the Bay Area Science Festival!

The Bay Area Science Festival, traditionally held in Oracle Park, has moved online this year! In collaboration with the UC Berkeley Pyle Group and SuperCDMS, SPS is excited to present three fun events as part of the 2020 Bay Area Science Festival. These events are free, interactive, and family-friendly!

These events will take approximately one hour. Check back here later for information on how to participate.

Squishy Circuits!

An interactive demo designed for 4th-5th graders teaching the fundamentals of circuits and how electricity moves.

Pyle Group Lab Tour!

A virtual lab tour, consisting of a live narration of 360° photos, mimicking the experience of being in person!

Ask a Berkeley Physicist!

A panel where anyone can ask a scientist questions both about being a physicist and physics itself!
