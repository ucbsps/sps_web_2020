Advocacy

Through Faculty-Student Lunches, Town Halls, and Outreach, SPS acts provides a voice for present and future physics undergraduates.

SPS helps build lifelong friendships through social meetings, our department wide BBQs, and working on committees with others.

Offering Mentorship, Study Halls, and Tutoring, SPS provides support for all of our journeys through physics.

Inspiration

With Undergraduate Seminars, involvement in the International Physics Tournament, and the new Berkeley Physics Tournament, SPS introduces students to more physics.
